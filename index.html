How the clock colors work

The outside face is the normal 1–12 clock. The inner ring shows 13–24 for afternoon/evening hours. The shaded wedges start from the center of the clock and fan outward: outer wedges show AM overlap, smaller inner wedges show PM/13–24 overlap. Green means you are inside a good shared window right now; pink means the next shared window is later.
